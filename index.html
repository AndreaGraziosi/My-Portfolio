interests

Andrea loves to learn how to code. Coding has opened a whole new world for Andrea, and she looks forward to creating technology for the benefit of all.

Andrea studies and teaches yoga in person and on zoom she studies a style of yoga developed by BKS Iyengar and taught by Ramanand Patel, which makes yoga accessible to all.

Andrea has a previous bachelors degree in Finance and International Business from Santa Clara University. Andrea has studied Art in Milan at Burgo school of design and completed an intensive 3 year training in Apparel Arts in San Francisco. Andrea loves to travel and has explored the world extensively. Andrea likes to learn new languages, the study of Advaita Vedanta and teachings of jiddu Krisnamurti.
